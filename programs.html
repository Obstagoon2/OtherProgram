FTC Team 8956 – Pyrogen

Founded: 2013

Pyrogen is our veteran FTC team, consistently competitive and known for strong engineering and control systems. Over the years, they’ve brought home numerous awards and advanced to the Louisiana Regional Championship multiple times.

  • 2024 Walker Qualifier – Design Award
  • New Orleans Qualifier – Design Award
  • 2023 LA Walker Qualifier – Control Award, Finalist Alliance Captain, Think Award 3rd Place
  • 2023 FTCLouisiana Hammond Qualifier – Control Award, Winning Alliance 1st Team Selected
  • 2023 Regional Championship – Finalist Alliance Captain, Innovate Award 2nd Place
  • 2022 LA Qualifier 1 – Finalist Alliance Captain, Inspire Award 3rd Place, Think Award
  • 2022 LA Qualifier 2 – Control Award 2nd Place, Think Award, Winning Alliance Captain
  • 2022 Louisiana Regional Championship – Motivate Award 2nd Place
  • 2021 LA New Orleans Qualifier – Control Award, Top Ranked 3rd Place
  • 2021 LA Northshore Qualifier – Design Award 3rd Place
  • 2021 Louisiana Regional Championship – Control Award, Design Award 3rd Place
  • 2020 LA FTC Qualifier #1 REMOTE – Innovate Award 3rd Place, Think Award, Top Ranked 2nd Place
  • 2019 Baton Rouge Qualifier – Control Award 3rd Place, Design Award
  • 2019 Northshore Qualifier – Control Award
  • 2019 Louisiana Regional Championship – Compass Award
