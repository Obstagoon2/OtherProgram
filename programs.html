Our FIRST Programs

Learn about our FTC and FLL programs — empowering students of all ages to explore STEM through hands-on robotics.

FTC Team 8956 – Pyrogen

Founded: 2014

Pyrogen has grown into a powerhouse FTC team in Slidell, competing in many seasons and earning awards for innovation, design, control, and more.

  • 2024 New Orleans Qualifier – Design Award
  • 2023 LA Walker Qualifier – Control Award; Think Award (3rd Place); Finalist Alliance – Captain
  • 2023 Hammond Qualifier – Control Award; Winning Alliance – 1st Team Selected
  • 2023 Louisiana Regional Championship – Finalist Alliance – Captain; Innovate Award (2nd Place)
  • 2022 LA Qualifier #1 – Finalist Alliance – Captain; Inspire Award (3rd Place); Think Award
  • 2022 LA Qualifier #2 – Control Award (2nd Place); Think Award; Winning Alliance – Captain
  • 2022 Louisiana Regional Championship – Motivate Award (2nd Place)

FTC Team 27406 – Impulse

Founded: 2024

Impulse is our rookie FTC team. In our first year, we focused on building skills and experience—even without award wins, we’re proud of our growth trajectory. Additionally, team member Zakk Jackson earned recognition as a Dean’s List Finalist.

  • 2024 Season (Rookie Year) – Gained foundational skills and competitive experience

FLL Team 65003 – Ignition

Founded: 2024

Ignition introduces younger students to robotics through the FIRST LEGO League Challenge. They competed in the 2024–2025 SUBMERGED season, advancing to the Louisiana State Championship and gaining teamwork and problem-solving experience.

  • 2024–2025 SUBMERGED Season – Advanced to Louisiana State Championship
